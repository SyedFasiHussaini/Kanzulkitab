Kanz-ul-kitab : Treasure of the Book (The Holy Qur'an) for all humanity.

An Online Not-for-profit Educational Institution Founded by Syed Fasiullah Hussaini (Software Developer).
Kanz-ul-kitab Educational Institution provides free online courses for everyone who wants to learn & understand Qur'an with Tajweed irrespective of their gender, caste, religion, nationality and also the family background they belongs to. It intents to connect its students to Allah (ﷻ), Prophet Muhammed(ﷺ), Ahlul Baith(رضی اللہ عنہ) & Awliyah Allah(رحمة الله عليه) as this will lead to an outcome of reflecting teachings of Qur'an & Prophet Muhammad(ﷺ) upon themselves.
Hence attaining discipline and good manners.

Courses Offered:

  • ‣ Qur'an with Tajweed

    In this course, we will teach you how to read the Holy Qur'an with proper Tajweed & Tarteel. We will begin with our first chapter, Arabic Alphabets (Mufradat/Huroof-e-Tahajji) with its articulation(Makharij). We will learn rules of Harkat, Huroof-e-Maddah, Huroof-e-Leen, Tanveen and Sakeen with ample of examples for each in our second chapter. Deep coverage of all the rules related to Tanveen and Noon-e-Sakeen including Izhaar and Ikhfaa. In our 4th chapter, We will learn Qalqalah, Tashdeed, Ra-e-Sakeen wa Ra-e-Mushaddadah, Huroof-e-Qamri wa Shamsi and Laam of Lafz-e-Allah in different cases. We will deep dive into Waqf rules, Different types of Madds, Meem-e-sakeen rules, Iqlab and Idghaam in our 5th chapter. In our final lesson, We will learn about Rasm-ul-Khat and Alaamat-e-Waqf. Finally, We will assess on all the rules you have learnt so far and based upon your performance, you will be graded and certified for this course.

  • ‣ Hifz-ul-Qur'an

    In this course, We will first assess you whether will you be able to read the Qur'an with Tajweed or not. If you not capable of reciting it as per the rules then we will first teach you Tajweed rules from scratch, Afterwards we'll move on and ask you to memorize it based on your own capability. You will have to recite atleast 1 chapter as a revision(Aamuqtah) on daily basis once you have completed memorizing atleast 1 chapter and started with the next. If you cooperate with us, it will not be challenging in online world as well.

    In this course, We will teach you proper Tajweed.
